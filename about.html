OUR HERITAGE

Our Heritage

Born in Saharanpur, Carving Royal Suites Globally

For over two decades, FurnishHut has stood at the intersection of traditional Indian woodcarving heritage and modern luxury ergonomics. Founded in the woodworking capital of Saharanpur, our atelier set out with a simple mission: to preserve the dying art of imperial Indian furniture carving and bring palace-worthy masterpieces into the world's most elite contemporary homes.

Every dining set, maharaja throne, pooja mandir, and tufted canopy headboard is carved from premium seasoned Teak and Rosewood. Our master woodcarvers spend up to 400 hours on a single frame, ensuring that no two pieces are ever completely identical.

We never duplicate molds or use structural plastic. Every component is engineered in heavy Teak wood with absolute design integrity.

Heirloom Longevity

Ethical Sourcing

Our hardwood is procured solely from sustainable Government timber farms, dried over three years to prevent structural warping.
